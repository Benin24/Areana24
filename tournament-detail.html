About This Tournament

BGMI Solo Showdown is Arena 24's flagship mobile battle royale tournament. Players compete individually across 4 rounds on Erangel and Miramar maps. Your total kill points and placement points across all rounds decide your rank on the leaderboard.

Top 10 players share the ₹10,000 prize pool. Results are posted within 2 hours of each round ending. All disputes are handled by Arena 24 admins.

Tournament Rules

1.All players must join the room exactly 10 minutes before match start. Latecomers will be disqualified.
2.Room ID and Password will be shared via email and dashboard 30 mins before match.
3.Emulators are strictly prohibited. Mobile devices only.
4.Hacking, teaming, or any unfair play results in immediate disqualification and account ban.
5.Scoring: +2 pts per kill, placement pts per zone finish.
6.Results are final. Screenshots of rank screen must be submitted within 15 mins after match.
7.Prize money transferred via UPI within 48 hours of tournament completion.
8.Arena 24 admin decisions are final in all disputes.
